About Us

The author and designer of this page is Cesar O. Torres Rodriguez, a 20 year old college student born in Lehigh County, Pennsylvania who has always had an interest in forensic science and crime documentaries. He created this website based on the well-known Netflix series Mindhunter. Cesar is a jack of all trades, he has interests in music, photography, basketball and technology, hes not afraid to get his hands dirty if hes feeling creative enough to test his own limits and he is always willing to try out new things. When the challenge of making a website came, Cesar initially wanted to create a basketball database, however, the idea simply didn't shape itself in his head and another idea emerged, the concept for the Crime Docs website. Crime Docs is Cesar's biggest body of work so far and hes very proud of it, it was designed and shaped together by him and it will continue to be molded by his ideas. The next step for him and his project is including his programmer friends in the project and get as much reliable help as possible. Cesar's discipline allowed him to stay focused on the idea and it allowed him to keep trying to make Crime Docs functional. He was taught to never crack under pressure and to remain composed when faced with adversity. That is why he never felt pressured or overwhelmed by the assignment of creating a website from scratch, because even if he doubted himself, he never stopped trying.
